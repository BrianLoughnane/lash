# Organization Settings

##### Specify global settings for how users will experience Atrius. Administrator access is required

Specify global settings for how users will experience Atrius. Administrator access is required.

To access Organization Settings, go to Settings on the left side menu, then Organization Settings.

**Organization Details**

- Organization name: The organization's full name.
- Organization short name: A shorter version of the organization's name, like an abbreviation, acronym, or omission of words "The", "Inc.", etc.
- Organization type: The organization's type, such as corporation, school, or government.
- Homepage default dashboard (optional): The organization's default selected dashboard as displayed on the Building Insights homepage.
- Units of measure: The organization's preference for imperial or metric units. Specifically, this affects the display of area and temperature units.
- Total energy: The organization's default combination of point types that represent total energy.
- Currency: The organization's default currency.
- First day of week: The organization's default work week. 'Monday' is selected by default.
- Fiscal year: The organization's fiscal year period start month.
- Emissions: The organization's emission rates settings for standard rate structures.
- Language: The organization's default language setting. Language determines the locale and date, time, and number formatting. Users may still specify their preferred language on their Profile.
- User analytics: The organization's default setting for determining how user activity may be tracked. Users may still specify their own tracking setting on their Profile.

**Organization name changes**

If you change your organization's name in Atrius, please contact Customer Support, so that we may update our internal records.

**Branding**

- Side menu color (optional): The organization's primary color, which will imbue the side menu and other parts of Atrius.
- Header logo: The organization's logo, which will be displayed in the header, at the top right of Atrius. Images must be .png or .jpg, 60px high, and at most 300px wide.
