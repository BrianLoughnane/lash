# Share a dashboard

##### Share dashboards, or individual cards, with your team or the public in a variety of ways

Share dashboards, or individual cards, with your team or the public in a variety of ways.

To access Dashboards, go to Dashboards on the left side menu, or Apps > Dashboards.

**Email a dashboard on demand or on a schedule**

To send an on-demand email, or to schedule an email for delivery, select Actions > Email dashboard.

In the Schedule email window, you can configure:
- Subject: Enter a subject line for the email message.
- Attachment type: Select the format in which the dashboard will be delivered as an email attachment: 'Link only', 'PDF and link', 'Image and link', or 'CSV and link'.
- Recipients: Select a recipient(s) from the list of existing Atrius users.
- Frequency: Select whether the email should be delivered immediately ('Once') or on a recurring basis ('Daily', 'Weekly', or 'Monthly').

When an email has been scheduled for daily, weekly, or monthly delivery, a record of the recurring email is stored in Apps > Scheduled Emails.

**Calendar views, rolling views, and custom periods in scheduled emails**

For scheduled emails, it is generally best to use standard calendar views and rolling views when specifying each card's time period, like 'Yesterday', 'Last month', and 'This year'. Individual year periods and custom periods do not dynamically update, resulting in dashboards that are "fixed" in time.

**Embed a link to a dashboard**

To acquire a publicly accessible link to a dashboard, select Actions > Dashboard settings. Set Public dashboard visibility to 'Enabled', then copy the Public dashboard direct link provided below. Select 'Save' when finished.

Every dashboard's public visibility is set to 'Disabled' by default.

**Embed a link to a card**

To share an individual card, hover over the card, open the dropdown menu at the top right, and select 'Share card'.

Set the card's public visibility to 'Enabled', then copy either the embed code or the direct link provided below.

**Data security in public dashboards and cards**

Public dashboards and cards have "obfuscated" URLs, meaning that they are unguessable and contain a string of random numbers and letters.

**Download a dashboard or card**

To download a dashboard, select Actions and one of 3 options: 'Download image', 'Download PDF', or 'Download CSV'. If CSV is selected, the downloaded ZIP file will consist of one CSV file per card.
