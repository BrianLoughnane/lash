# Estimated points

Auto-generate annual estimates for electricity and natural gas consumption based on an EUI input.

Updated over 2 months ago

Estimated points can help your organization fill gaps in portfolio data or they can be used as a benchmarking tool. Gaps may be attributed to blockers in data collection, for example, organizations with leased sites, that are unable to access utility data or have no control over sub-metering. As a benchmark, estimated points can be used as an overlay to your actual electricity or natural gas use.

Estimated points allow you to generate an annual energy consumption value for your building based on the buildings area, and an EUI of your choice. For U.S. buildings we support the Commercial Buildings Energy Conservation Survey (CBECS) database from the Energy Information Administration (EIA)\*, to provide average EUI's based on building type and size. The EUI you select will then be multiplied by your building's area, to calculate an annual energy consumption value.

\*CBECS' latest release in December 2022, provides average EUI's based on 2018 building energy data, which is used to power this feature.

#### Enabling Estimated points

**Building Profile**
* For adding or editing estimated points one building at a time.
* In the Building Profile, navigate to 'Estimated points,' to enable points for Estimated electricity or Estimated natural gas. Based on the building's location you may be able to select from the following options to generate your EUI:
  
  + Principal activity type (CBECS): Select from a list of principal activity types for your building to determine the average EUI based on building type and region. Limited to buildings in the U.S.
  + Square footage (CBECS): Use an average EUI based on your building's area and region. Limited to buildings in the U.S.
  + Custom EUI: Enter a pre-determined EUI of your choice.

**Batch Building Edit**
* For adding or editing estimated points for batches of up to 500 buildings.
* From the buildings table, select the buildings in where wish to add estimated points, and from the Actions dropdown select 'Edit buildings.' Navigate to the last 5 columns:
  
  + Estimated electricity: Select from the same options outlined above (availability dependent on building location).
  + Custom EUI: If 'Custom' was selected for 'Estimated electricity' enter your custom EUI in kwh /square foot. Otherwise leave blank.
  + Estimated natural gas: Select from the same options outlined above (availability dependent on building location).
  + Custom EUI: If 'Custom' was selected for 'Estimated natural gas' enter your custom EUI in cubic feet /square foot. Otherwise leave blank.
  + Principal activity type: If 'Principal activity type' was selected for either electricity or natural gas, select your building's type from the dropdown options.
